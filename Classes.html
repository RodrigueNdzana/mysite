Group Exercise Classes

Our expert instructors will guide you through new routines designed to get you moving in new and exciting ways. Our group exercise classes are a fun and effective way to work out and help you reach your exercise goals. Come and try something new.

Classes

Grid Training

Grid Training is our high intensity, interval programme designed to build strength, stability, stamina and team spirit.

Includes: Grid Training

Yoga

Yoga for everyone (yes, even you!). Our classes focus on aligning and strengthening your body and restoring a sense of calm.

Includes: Yoga Calm, Yoga Strength, Yoga Align

Cycle

Cycle takes outdoor cycling principles and presents them to you in an indoor studio. Let the music set the pace as you boost your fitness levels with time in the saddle.

Includes: Ride

Dance and Conditioning

Choreographed routines that use elements of dance to make you sweat, sway and smile while you shake it off.

Includes: Pound, Zumba, Core Conditioning, Shape, Step

Boxing

We use the basic principles of combat to create fun, fast-paced classes to KO calories, build stamina and endurance and allow you to blow off some steam.

Includes: Kick, Boxing, Rumble
